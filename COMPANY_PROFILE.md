\# PROJECT BRIEF

\#\# Project

Design and develop the official website for \*\*Panchsheel Geo Infra Solution\*\*.

This is not a startup landing page.

This is the digital identity of a professional engineering and geospatial surveying company that wants to win trust from government departments, EPC contractors, infrastructure companies, mining organizations, renewable energy firms, and industrial clients.

Every design decision should reinforce technical competence, professionalism, precision, and long-term credibility.

\---

\# Company Overview

Panchsheel Geo Infra Solution is an Indian geospatial and infrastructure surveying company headquartered in Bhopal, Madhya Pradesh.

The company operates across India and provides professional surveying solutions using modern surveying technologies including DGPS, Drone Surveying, Total Station, LiDAR, GIS, and advanced geospatial workflows.

The company focuses on delivering highly accurate survey data that supports infrastructure development, engineering projects, construction planning, transportation, mining operations, renewable energy projects, and government initiatives.

The company believes in precision, reliability, professionalism, and timely execution.

\# Design Philosophy

The website should feel closer to:

\- Engineering consultancy  
\- Infrastructure company  
\- Surveying expert  
\- Technical organization

Not:

\- SaaS startup  
\- AI company  
\- Crypto company  
\- Marketing agency

\---

\# Visual Direction

The design language should be:

Minimal but authoritative.

Structured.

Grid-based.

Editorial.

Purposeful.

Generous whitespace.

Large photography.

Strong typography.

Restrained animations.

Excellent visual hierarchy.

Avoid decorative elements that do not support communication.

\---

\# Color Direction

Prefer neutral colors inspired by engineering and infrastructure.

Suggested palette direction:

Deep charcoal

Steel gray

Concrete

Off-white

Muted earth tones

One disciplined accent color.

Avoid colorful gradients.

Avoid startup color palettes.

\---

\# Typography

Typography should feel engineered rather than trendy.

Use a carefully selected display font paired with an excellent body font.

Hierarchy should communicate authority.

Readability is more important than novelty.

\# Interaction Philosophy

Interactions should feel subtle and professional.

Animations should support usability.

\---

\# Copywriting Style

Write like experienced engineers.

Use specific language.

Avoid marketing clichés.

Avoid exaggerated claims.

Avoid unnecessary adjectives.

Clarity is more valuable than cleverness.

Technical accuracy builds trust.

\---

\# Technical Requirements

Use:

\- Next.js  
\- React  
\- TypeScript  
\- Tailwind CSS

Code should be production ready.

Responsive by default.

Accessible.

SEO friendly.

Performance optimized.

Maintainable.

\---

\# Success Criteria

The website should not look AI generated.

It should feel as though it was designed by an experienced creative agency specializing in engineering and industrial companies.

Every design decision should have a clear purpose.

The final result should communicate confidence, precision, and professionalism while remaining modern, elegant, and memorable.

\---

\# Final Instruction

Before writing code:

Understand the business.

Understand the audience.

Understand the industry.

Understand the competition.

Only then begin designing.

Never sacrifice clarity for visual effects.

Never sacrifice professionalism for trendiness.

Design something that will still feel timeless five years from now.
